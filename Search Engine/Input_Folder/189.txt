Subscribe to our newsletter by following our publication on Medium! We are on a mission to get the best content relevant to data science to everyone. We strive to present well-written, informative articles that our audience is excited to read. Since we started, we have been amazed by the quality of the posts that we receive. We can feel the hard work and the willingness to share something important and we feel privileged to help and support our contributors to get their message across. Ultimately, we hope to make the world a better place and we believe that publishing and sharing data-related knowledge written by passionate and knowledgable authors is a crucial part of achieving this goal. Subscribing to our newsletter will help ensure that you get that knowledge delivered right to your inbox in our Monthly Editions and Weekly Digests. Monthly Edition: Every month, one person from our team selects a topic and includes the articles he or she thought were the most exciting, innovative, and thought-provoking. You can find our previous monthly editions here. Weekly Digest: Starting September 20, 2019, our Weekly Digest replaced our Weekly Selection. Our digest features personalized recommendations for you and is delivered directly to your inbox. It’s easy to subscribe. Just follow our publication by clicking the “Follow” button in the rectangle to the left and turn on “receive letters” in your settings. You can easily unsubscribe on your settings page at any time. We’re always looking for ideas to make our newsletters the best that they can be, so if you have any feedback, please don’t hesitate to reach out. Thanks!
